Videography

Videography has been a unique creative outlet for me. Immersing myself in the world of video, I was immediately drawn to its dynamic storytelling possibilities. In my films, I focus on illuminating the mundane, visible in the short film ‘De Fles.’ Today, I strive to continue exploring the art of videography, with a particular emphasis on light, scenery, and camera work.

This project was created as an unofficial music video for the band Donna Blue, I was responsible for the lighting on set. Full credits can be found in the Youtube description. We concluded the project with a screening in Amsterdam, in collaboration with Creature Magazine.

In this brief film, I aimed to capture the essence of freedom that cycling embodies for me: the boundless roads stretching toward the horizon and the liberating landscapes. This project was solely made by me.
